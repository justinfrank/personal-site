## Part 1

I don't know where this is going to go, but it's really a way for me to dump the catalog of history from my brain of the things I've learned and done while helping to build a design system that supports multiple brands, audiences, and platforms in a industry focused on providing support for people who have lost loved ones. 

### Context

I work in the funeral industry. I talk about death everyday. I never thought this is where I'd be but I also have never been more empathetic to the goal that I work towards every day.
